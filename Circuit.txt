HC-SR04 Ultrasonic
        ------------------------
        VCC → 5V (Arduino)
        GND → GND
        TRIG → D9
        ECHO → D10

          LEDs
        ------------------------
        LED1 → D4 → (220Ω) → GND
        LED2 → D5 → (220Ω) → GND
        LED3 → D6 → (220Ω) → GND

          Buzzer
        ------------------------
        Buzzer + → D7
        Buzzer - → GND

        Arduino Powered by USB
